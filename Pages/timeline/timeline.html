1. Yarn Selection
High-quality yarn is selected to make the socks.
2. Knitting
The yarn is knitted into socks using special machines.
3. Dyeing
The socks are dyed in various colors and patterns.
4. Linking
The toe part of each sock is linked together neatly.
5. Quality Check
Each sock is checked carefully for any defects.
6. Packaging
Finally, socks are packed and ready for delivery.
